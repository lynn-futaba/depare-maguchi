トリガー Toriga Trigger
-------------------------
・生産数は設備から信号入れてもらう（＋1）
Seisan-sū wa setsubi kara shingō irete morau (+1).
The production count will be incremented (+1) based on a signal received from the equipment.

・生産数＋１で在庫数計算
Seisan-sū +1 de zaiko-sū keisan.
When the production count increases by +1, the inventory quantity will be recalculated.

・デパレシステムから供給で在庫数計算
De-pare shisutemu kara kyōkyū de zaiko-sū keisan.
The inventory quantity will also be recalculated when supply comes from the depalletizing system.

トランザクション領域 Toranzakushon ryōiki transaction area 
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 Master Tables (マスター)

Purpose: Store relatively static or reference data that defines the core entities of the system.
Characteristics:

Data changes rarely (e.g., product info, equipment info, customer info).
Acts as a lookup or reference for other tables.
Examples:

Product_Master (Product ID, Name, Specs)
Equipment_Master (Equipment ID, Location)
Customer_Master (Customer ID, Name)

Usage: Used to maintain consistency and avoid duplication across transactions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
✅ Transaction Tables (トランザクション)

Purpose: Store dynamic, operational data generated by business processes.
Characteristics:

Data changes frequently (e.g., production count, inventory movements, sales orders).
Represents events or activities over time.
Examples:

Production_Transaction (Timestamp, Equipment ID, Quantity)
Inventory_Transaction (Timestamp, Product ID, In/Out Quantity)
Sales_Order (Order Date, Customer ID, Amount)

Usage: Used for reporting, auditing, and real-time operations.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Key Difference

Master = Who/What (static definitions)
Transaction = When/How Much (dynamic events)
